Who We Are

We are a team of passionate engineers, crafting intelligent machines that elevate human-robot collaboration. Working at the frontiers of technology, we harness advancements in AI and robotics to create systems that seamlessly interact with the physical world. Taking care of the heavy lifting, so you can focus on what you do best.

Our Vision

To contribute in providing robotic companions in Industries, Society, Homes and in future even Space. We envision a world evolving into a new era of seamless human-robot collaboration, unlocking greater possibilities and empowering people to achieve more than ever before.

Robotics in a nutshell is triptych of Mechanical, Electronics & Computer Science. Apart from building robots, we’re building a culture of innovation, synergy, and relentless curiosity. Our core values lie in Bold R&D, and crafting meaningful products that serve.
